# Introduction to Climate Change Mitigation

Welcome to this comprehensive guide on climate change mitigation! This post will help you understand key strategies and solutions.

![Climate Solutions Overview](./images/climate-overview.png)

## What is Climate Mitigation?

Climate mitigation refers to efforts to reduce or prevent emission of greenhouse gases. It involves implementing new technologies, changing management practices, and modifying human behavior.

### Key Mitigation Strategies

There are several major approaches:

1. **Renewable Energy** - Transitioning to solar, wind, and other clean energy sources
2. **Energy Efficiency** - Reducing energy consumption through better technology and practices
3. **Carbon Sequestration** - Removing CO2 from the atmosphere through natural and technological means

![Mitigation Strategies Diagram](./images/mitigation-strategies.png)

Key Steps:

## Key Concepts

- **Carbon Footprint**: Total greenhouse gas emissions caused by an individual, event, or organization
- **Net Zero**: Achieving balance between emissions produced and emissions removed from atmosphere
- **Climate Resilience**: Ability to anticipate, prepare for, and adapt to climate impacts
- **Just Transition**: Ensuring climate action supports workers and communities affected by change
